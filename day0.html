Queen's Facilities and Amenities

Wi-fi

Euduroam is available. If you do not have access to euduroam, please contact the Queen's organizers at least two weeks before the meeting so we can facilitate guest accounts to be set up on the main Queen's wi-fi system.

Nuring Parents/Quiet Room

We have a Nursing Parents Room/Quiet Room (on the first basement level of Main Physics) that can be utilised by visitors as a lactation room or as a space for anyone who needs a quiet non-stimulating environment for a short period of time. There are couches/soft chairs in the room and and a restroom is located nearby. No refirgerator is allowed in room, but there is a communal refrigerator located on the ground up. Please contact the organisers to reserve this space during the meeting.

COVID-19 Protocols and Masking Policy

There is clear emperical data that mask wearing and airflow minimise the spread of COVID-19 virus in indoor gatherings and helps protect those with pre-existing conditions that may be exacerbated by a COVID infection. We ask that attendees use N95, KN95, KF94, or FFP2 masks (or their equivalents) in the meeting spaces, except when consuming food or beverages. The organisers will provide these masks for free for any who need them. Presenters will be allowed to remove their masks during their presentations, and members of the audience asking questions will be able to lower their masks to speak as needed.

If you do test positive for COVID-19 during or shortly after the meeting, please let the organisers know so we can let attendees know about the possible exposure.

Queen's Facilities and Amenities

Wi-fi

Eduroam is available. If you do not have access to eduroam, please contact the Queen's organizers at least two weeks before the meeting so we can facilitate guest accounts to be set up on the main Queen's wi-fi system.

Nuring Parents/Quiet Room

We have a Nursing Parents Room/Quiet Room (on the first basement level of Main Physics) that can be utilised by visitors as a lactation room or as a space for anyone who needs a quiet non-stimulating environment for a short period of time. There are couches/soft chairs in the room and and a restroom is located nearby. No refirgerator is allowed in room, but there is a communal refrigerator located on the ground up. Please contact the organisers to reserve this space during the meeting.
